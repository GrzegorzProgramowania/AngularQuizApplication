AngularQuizApplication
The quiz is built in Angular and TypeScript using additional libraries like Bootstrap and RxJs. In the quiz, there will be approximately 20 questions, and for each question, there will be 4 answer options, with one of them being correct.

Rules:

Correct answer gives you 10 points.
Incorrect answer gives you -10 points.
You will have 60s to anwer each question.
