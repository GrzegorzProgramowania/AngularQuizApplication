The quiz is built in Angular and TypeScript using additional libraries like Bootstrap and RxJs. In the quiz, there will be approximately 30 questions, and for each question, there will be 4 answer options, with one of them being correct.
There will also be the option to choose difficulty levels. 
Rules:
Easy mode: 30 seconds, +1 point for correct answer,
Normal mode: 20 seconds, +1.25 points,
Hard mode: 10 second, +1,5 points
